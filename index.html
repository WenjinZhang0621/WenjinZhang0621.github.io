Hi, everyone! Welcome to my homepage.

I am currently a senior student in the School of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), majoring in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics. Along with my undergraduate studies, I have worked on three main research projects. Under the guidance of Professor Weijing Tang (Carnegie Mellon University) and Professor Yinqiu He (University of Wisconsin-Madison), I investigated network community size inference using spectral methods. I also collaborated with Professor Yuqi Gu (Columbia University) to extend the Stepwise Goodness-of-Fit (StGoF) method for mixture models and spectral clustering. Additionally, I worked with Professor Yuqi Gu and Professor Yixin Wang (University of Michigan) on causal discovery for latent attributes, leveraging scalable algorithms for theoretical and empirical analysis. Currently, I am co-authoring a working manuscript on the StGoF method.
