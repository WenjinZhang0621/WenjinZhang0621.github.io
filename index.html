Greetings! Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently a senior student in the School of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), majoring in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics. I am broadly interested in various fields of statistics and statistical methods, aiming to balance rigorous mathematical proofs with practical application effectiveness.

Since my junior year, I have been guided by Professor at Columbia University on extending the Stepwise Goodness-of-Fit (StGoF) method from degree-corrected block models to sub-exponential mixtures. Additionally, I have also been mentored by Professor Yuqi Gu and Professor at the University of Michigan on causal discovery for latent attributes. I have also been advised by Professor at Carnegie Mellon University and Professor at the University of Wisconsin-Madison on inference on community size.
